About Block-Level Elements

Block-level elements occupy the entire width of their container and begin on a new line. Common examples include <div> and <p>.

Features of Block-Level Elements

  • Always start on a new line.
  • Can contain inline elements and other block-level elements.
<div>This is a block-level container</div>
"Block-level elements provide structure and separation in your web page."
